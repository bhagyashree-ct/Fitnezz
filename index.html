At Fitnezz, we believe that fitness is not just a routine, but a lifestyle. Whether you’re a beginner or a seasoned athlete, Fitnezz offers a variety of workouts, nutrition plans, and wellness tips tailored to your unique needs.

Basic Plan

  • Price: ₹5,000/month

  • Description: For beginners looking to get started with fitness at an affordable rate.

  • Feature:

    1. Access to basic workout plans (e.g., bodyweight exercises, beginner routines)

    2. Nutrition tips and healthy meal suggestions

    3. Access to a community forum

    4. Monthly progress tracking

    5. Email support

    6. 1 new workout plan per month

Standard Plan

  • Price: ₹10,000/month

  • Aimed at regular gym-goers who want more variety and structure

  • Feature:

    1. Everything in the Basic Plan

    2. Access to intermediate-level workout plans (e.g., weightlifting, cardio, and strength training)

    3. Personalized meal plans (weekly updates)

    4. Weekly progress tracking

    5. Access to live fitness classes (e.g., yoga, HIIT)

    6. Mobile app integration for easy tracking

Premium Plan

  • Price: ₹15,000/month

  • For those serious about their fitness goals, offering personalized coaching and extensive resources.

  • Feature:

    1. Everything in the Standard Plan

    2. Access to advanced-level workout plans (e.g., hypertrophy training, sports-specific routines)

    3. One-on-one coaching sessions (monthly check-ins)

    4. Customized workout and nutrition plans tailored to specific goals

    5. Daily progress tracking and feedback from coaches

    6. Exclusive access to premium webinars and workshops
